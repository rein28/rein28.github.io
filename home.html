~ Ka wheako i nga tikanga Māori ma te kai ~

(Experience the Māori culture through food)


THE HAUKAI RESTAURANT

Haukai Restaurant specialises in Māori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands.
The dinner menu features Māori and Pākeha kai options and drinks.
We want our guests to experience Te Ao Māori with the use of karakia for
blessing food and other Māori culture. The town is also near The Lord of the Rings site.
We hope to see you soon and experience the Māori culture through food.
